Public Defense of a Research Scholar

Ms. Uzma Munshi, a Ph.D. Research Scholar of Area Study Centre, has successfully defended her PhD. Dissertation titled "STRATEGIC REALIGNMENT BETWEEN RUSSIA, CHINA AND PAKISTAN: CHALLENGES AND OPPORTUNITIES FOR PAKISTAN" under the supervision of Prof. Hussain Shaheed Soherwordi. Her External Examiners were Brig (Rtd.) Dr. Saif Ur Rehman and Prof. Muhammad Khan whereas the Internal Examiner was Prof. Shabir Ahmad Khan.
